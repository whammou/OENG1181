Undertake a Risk Assessment

  • Risk assessment is a critical part of a risk management
  • Involves:
    • Identifying the risk
    • Analysing the risk
    • Evaluating the risk

  • Identify:
    • Sources of risk
    • Causes of risk
    • Potential consequence
    • External and internal

  • Analyse:
    • Using quantified levels of Likelihood and consequence

  • > 12 E: Extreme; immediate action required

Managing Risk

  • Manage
    • Modify and implement
  • Principles
    • Communicate and consult
    • Eliminate
      • Evaluate option A vs option B
    • Reduce likelihood
      • Training & education
      • Preventative maintenance
      • Auditing programs
      • Quality assurance, inspection and testing
      • Process change
    • Reduce Consequence
      • Personal protective equipment (PPE)
      • Barriers
      • Recovery plans
    • Share risk
    • Take on risk

Monitor and Review

  • Document the process
  • Review strategies for risk management
  • Check that ll risks have been identified
